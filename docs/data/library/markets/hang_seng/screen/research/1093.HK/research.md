## INVESTMENT THESIS
For a value investor, CSPC presents a rare combination in Chinese healthcare: balance-sheet fortitude, shareholder returns, and reasonable multiples against a platform that is beginning to monetise innovation externally. The screen’s Graham Enterprising, Neff PEGY, Economic Moat, Dividend Growth, Composite Value, and Financial Health passes align with observable fundamentals in the Yahoo dataset—net cash orientation, heavy but funded R&D, and consistent dividend distributions (RMB 2.5bn paid in FY2025 per cash-flow statement). Business quality is evidenced by scale (RMB 26.0bn revenue in FY2025), gross margins still above 65%, and negligible financial debt relative to RMB 34.6bn total equity. The value case is not purely statistical: outbound licensing to AstraZeneca (obesity/metabolic, eight programmes) and Madrigal (oral GLP-1, July 2025) introduces milestone-driven upside that traditional earnings screens under-weight. That said, normalised earnings have fallen materially since FY2022, so the buy signal rests on the view that current multiples already embed disappointment in legacy products (likely volume-based procurement and mix shift) while optionality in biologics, ADCs, and metabolic disease is under-priced. The screen’s “new” one-week signal and 0% conviction score warrant phased accumulation rather than aggressive sizing until interim filing confirmation arrives.
